I volunteered as a photographer for _Afternoon with the Arts_, an annual event in Fort Smith that brings together local artists, performers, and the community. My role was to capture the energy and creativity of the event through photography, ensuring that key moments, live performances, and artist showcases were well-documented.

Throughout the event, I focused on taking both candid and staged shots, highlighting the excitement of attendees and the hard work of the artists. I worked closely with the event organizers to ensure I didn’t miss any important moments, such as award presentations, speeches, and interactive art exhibits. Seeing the joy on people’s faces as they admired the art and performances made the experience incredibly rewarding.

After the event, my photos were used for social media promotion, marketing materials, and press releases to help spread the word and build excitement for future events. Volunteering in this way allowed me to contribute to the local arts scene while also doing something I love—capturing moments that tell a story.
