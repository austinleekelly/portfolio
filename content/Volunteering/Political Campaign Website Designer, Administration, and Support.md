I volunteered to support a former boss from the [[Fort Smith Chamber of Commerce - Directory of Information and Statistics]] in running and administering the digital presence for an Arkansas State Representative campaign. 

Drawing from my background in web administration, digital marketing, and IT support, I helped develop and maintain the campaign website to ensure a professional, accessible, and informative platform for voters.

My responsibilities included:

- **[[Website and Content Management|Website Management]]:** Designed, updated, and maintained the campaign [[Content Management Software|Wordpress]] website, ensuring it effectively communicated the candidate’s message, policies, and upcoming events.
- **[[Content Strategy and Planning|Content]] Updates:** Managed blog posts, press releases, and multimedia content to keep supporters informed and engaged.
- **Technical Support:** Ensured site security, uptime, and responsiveness across desktop and mobile devices.
- **Digital Strategy Support:** Assisted in crafting an online presence that aligned with the candidate’s values and campaign goals.

I chose to contribute to this campaign because I had firsthand experience working with the candidate and strongly aligned with their character and values. My goal was to help them make a greater societal impact by ensuring their message reached the community effectively.
