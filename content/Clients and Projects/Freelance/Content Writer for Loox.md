## 📌 Summary

As a freelance case study writer, I was tasked with highlighting how a Shopify brand could integrate Loox to improve the customer journey and maximize paid traffic efficiency. The piece focused on showing how social proof can be used across landing pages and email flows to build trust, reduce buyer hesitation, and streamline conversions. My approach emphasized clear storytelling, strategic framing, and practical implementation to help Loox stand out to potential users.

## 🎯 Key Responsibilities

- **Case Study Development** – Wrote a full-length case study that walked through a potential customer journey, showing how Loox could be embedded at critical points to increase trust and support conversions.
    
- **Strategic Framing** – Positioned Loox as a powerful tool for maximizing ROI from existing paid traffic by leveraging customer reviews across landing pages and post-purchase emails.
    
- **Customer Journey Mapping** – Highlighted opportunities for using Loox before, during, and after purchase to build credibility and support long-term customer relationships.
    
- **Marketing-Ready Content** – Delivered the case study as polished, client-ready content that could be used in Loox’s sales collateral, blog, or onboarding materials.
    
- **Voice & Tone Matching** – Adapted to the brand's tone while maintaining clarity and persuasive messaging throughout the case study.
