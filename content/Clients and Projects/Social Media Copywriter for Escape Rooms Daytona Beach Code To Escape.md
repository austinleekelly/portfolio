## 📌 Summary

I worked as a freelance Social Media Copywriter for a Florida-based escape room brand, focusing on promoting their corporate team-building packages. The goal was to show businesses how escape rooms could improve communication, boost morale, and foster teamwork. I created posts that were fun yet professional to appeal to both HR managers and everyday users.

## 📂 Role Overview

- **💼 Company:** Escape Rooms Daytona Beach (Code to Escape)
    
- **📆 Duration:** December 2019
    
- **🏢 Employment Type:** Freelance
    
- **📍 Location:** Remote
    

## 🎯 Key Responsibilities

- **Social Media Copywriting** - Wrote short, engaging posts for Facebook and Instagram to promote corporate events and private bookings.
    
- **Brand Tone Alignment** - Used a tone that was playful but still professional to connect with business leaders and local customers.
    
- **Value Proposition Framing** - Showed how escape rooms can be used for team-building by promoting communication, problem-solving, and collaboration.
    
- **Call-to-Action Optimization** - Added soft CTAs to encourage bookings without sounding too pushy.
    
- **Event Promotion** - Created both timely and evergreen posts to support seasonal promotions and special offers.
    
---

## 🚀 Achievements and Impact

- ✅ **Increased inquiries for corporate bookings** through targeted social media posts that highlighted escape rooms as effective team-building tools.
    
- ✅ **Boosted engagement** on Facebook and Instagram by creating content that resonated with both business leaders and the local community.
    
- ✅ **Helped position the brand** as a top choice for corporate events, supporting its growth in the local market.
    
- ✅ **Created evergreen content** that continued to generate interest and inquiries long after initial posts were made.
