## 🎭 Afterschool Program Improv Comedy Teacher - Future School

At Future School of Fort Smith, I volunteered as an afterschool Improv Comedy Instructor, leading a high school classroom through Improv Comedy 101. My focus was on helping students build confidence, creativity, and communication skills through on-stage sketch comedy and scene work.

Through a mix of interactive exercises, improv games, and performance techniques, I helped students break out of their comfort zones, think on their feet, and embrace teamwork. By emphasizing yes, and thinking, quick decision-making, and active listening, I provided a space where students could explore self-expression in a fun, judgment-free environment.

Key takeaways from my work: 
* ✅ Confidence Building – Encouraged students to take risks and own their presence on stage. 
* ✅ Improved [[Customer Service and Communication|Communication]] – Strengthened their ability to adapt and engage in conversations naturally. 
* ✅ Team [[Collaboration and Teamwork|Collaboration]] – Taught the importance of trust and support in group performances. 
* ✅ Creative [[Problem Solving and Critical Thinking|Problem-Solving]] – Helped students develop spontaneity and think outside the box.

This experience reinforced my passion for teaching, public speaking, and creative storytelling, and I loved seeing students grow both on and off the stage.

## 💻 Political Campaign Website Designer, Administration, and Support

I volunteered to support a former boss from the [[Fort Smith Chamber of Commerce - Directory of Information and Statistics]] in running and administering the digital presence for an Arkansas State Representative campaign. 

Drawing from my background in web administration, digital marketing, and IT support, I helped develop and maintain the campaign website to ensure a professional, accessible, and informative platform for voters.

My responsibilities included:

- **[[Website and Content Management|Website Management]]:** Designed, updated, and maintained the campaign [[Content Management Software|Wordpress]] website, ensuring it effectively communicated the candidate’s message, policies, and upcoming events.
- **[[Writing and Marketing#Content Strategy and Planning|Content]] Updates:** Managed blog posts, press releases, and multimedia content to keep supporters informed and engaged.
- **Technical Support:** Ensured site security, uptime, and responsiveness across desktop and mobile devices.
- **Digital Strategy Support:** Assisted in crafting an online presence that aligned with the candidate’s values and campaign goals.

I chose to contribute to this campaign because I had firsthand experience working with the candidate and strongly aligned with their character and values. My goal was to help them make a greater societal impact by ensuring their message reached the community effectively.

## 📸 Photographer - Afternoon with the Arts

I volunteered as a photographer for _Afternoon with the Arts_, an annual event in Fort Smith that brings together local artists, performers, and the community. My role was to capture the energy and creativity of the event through photography, ensuring that key moments, live performances, and artist showcases were well-documented.

Throughout the event, I focused on taking both candid and staged shots, highlighting the excitement of attendees and the hard work of the artists. I worked closely with the event organizers to ensure I didn’t miss any important moments, such as award presentations, speeches, and interactive art exhibits. Seeing the joy on people’s faces as they admired the art and performances made the experience incredibly rewarding.

After the event, my photos were used for social media promotion, marketing materials, and press releases to help spread the word and build excitement for future events. Volunteering in this way allowed me to contribute to the local arts scene while also doing something I love - capturing moments that tell a story.
