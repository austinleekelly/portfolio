1. **Browse by Institution**: Certifications are grouped under the organization that issued them (e.g., [[Certifications/Department of Homeland Security|Department of Homeland Security]], [[Digital Marketer/|Digital Marketer]], [[Klaviyo/|Klaviyo]]). 2. **Use Tags for Filtering**: Each certification is tagged based on its focus area (e.g., #IT, #Cybersecurity, #Marketing). Use these tags to quickly find certifications relevant to your interests or needs.

3. **Use Search**: Looking for something specific? Use the search bar to find keywords, industries, platforms, or project types across all sections.
