## 📌 Summary

The **Scrum Fundamentals Certified (SFC™)** course, offered by SCRUMstudy, provides individuals with a foundational understanding of the Scrum framework. This certification is ideal for those new to Agile methodologies, aiming to grasp key Scrum concepts and practices. Upon completion, participants will have the knowledge to contribute effectively to Scrum teams and projects. ​

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** Scrum Fundamentals Certified (SFC™)​
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:** [SCRUMstudy](https://www.scrumstudy.com/)​
- **📆 Date Earned:** 10/31/2017
- **📜 Certification Level:** Beginner
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **Scrum Framework Understanding** – Gain insights into the principles and processes of Scrum, including roles, events, and artifacts.
    
- **Scrum Implementation** – Learn how to apply Scrum practices to enhance project delivery and team collaboration. 
    
- **Agile Mindset Adoption** – Develop an Agile mindset to adapt to changing project requirements and improve responsiveness. ​
    

## 🚀 Skills Acquired

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:**
    
    - Understanding of Scrum roles, events, and artifacts.
    - Ability to participate effectively in Scrum teams.​
    - Knowledge of Scrum principles and how they contribute to project success.​
        
- **Soft Skills:**
    
    - Enhanced team collaboration and communication.​
        
    - Adaptability to Agile project environments.​
        
    - Commitment to continuous learning and professional development.
        

### 💻 **Tools & Technologies**

- **Scrum Framework** – A lightweight framework that helps people, teams, and organizations generate value through adaptive solutions for complex problems.
