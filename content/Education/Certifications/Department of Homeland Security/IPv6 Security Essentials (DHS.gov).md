## 📌 Summary

_The IPv6 Security Essentials course provides an in-depth look at IPv6 architecture, related protocols, and the unique security challenges it presents. The training also introduces mitigation strategies for vulnerabilities associated with IPv6 implementation, including transition mechanisms and protocol-level threats._

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** IPv6 Security Essentials
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:** [Department of Homeland Security](https://www.dhs.gov/)
    
- **📆 Date Earned:** 01/08/2018
    
- **📜 Certification Level:** Advanced
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **IPv6 Fundamentals** – _Understand IPv6 addressing and its deployment status across networks._
    
- **Security Threats** – _Identify and assess common IPv6-specific security threats involving ICMPv6, DHCPv6, and DNSv6._
    
- **Mitigation Strategies** – _Learn defense strategies for securing IPv6-enabled environments and managing transition mechanisms securely._
    

## 🚀 Skills Acquired

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:** IPv6 networking, security threat identification, protocol analysis, secure implementation
    
- **Soft Skills:** Analytical thinking, protocol evaluation, risk assessment
    

### 💻 **Tools & Technologies**

- **IPv6 Networking Protocols** – _ICMPv6, DHCPv6, DNSv6, IPv6 Transition Mechanisms_
    
- **Security Management Tools** – _Monitoring and defensive strategy planning for IPv6 networks_
