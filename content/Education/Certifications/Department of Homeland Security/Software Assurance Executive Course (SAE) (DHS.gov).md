## 📌 Summary

_This course is tailored for executives and managers who wish to deepen their understanding of software assurance, specifically related to acquisition and development. It focuses on software security assurance practices and resources available to address challenges across the acquisition and development life cycles._

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** Software Assurance Executive Course (SAE)
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:** [Department of Homeland Security](https://www.dhs.gov/)
    
- **📆 Date Earned:** 12/27/2017
    
- **📜 Certification Level:** Intermediate
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **Software Assurance Practices and Challenges** - _Learn about current practices and challenges in software assurance._
    
- **Software Supply Chain Risk Management** - _Gain insights into managing risks within the software supply chain._
    
- **Agile Methods and Software Trustworthiness** - _Understand agile methods and the importance of adopting software trustworthiness._
    

## 🚀 Skills Acquired

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:** Software assurance practices, risk management, agile methodologies
    
- **Soft Skills:** Strategic planning, executive-level decision-making
    

### 💻 **Tools and Technologies**

- **Software Assurance Resources** - _Utilize resources to address software security challenges._
    
- **Risk Management Frameworks** - _Apply frameworks for managing software supply chain risks._
