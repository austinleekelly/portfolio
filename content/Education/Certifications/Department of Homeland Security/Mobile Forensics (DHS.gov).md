## 📌 Summary

_The Mobile Forensics course provides an in-depth overview of mobile forensics, focusing on the extraction and analysis of evidence from mobile devices. It highlights the significance of mobile devices in investigations, with an emphasis on iOS architecture and forensic techniques for data acquisition and analysis._

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** Mobile Forensics
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:** [Department of Homeland Security](https://www.dhs.gov/)
    
- **📆 Date Earned:** 12/29/2017
    
- **📜 Certification Level:** Advanced
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **Mobile Devices in Investigations** - _Understand the impact of mobile devices in modern investigations._
    
- **iOS Architecture Basics** - _Learn the fundamental filesystem, operating system, and security architecture of iOS devices._
    
- **Acquisition and Analysis Techniques** - _Master tools and techniques for acquiring and analyzing data from iOS devices._
    

## 🚀 Skills Acquired

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:** Mobile forensics, iOS device analysis, data extraction
    
- **Soft Skills:** Attention to detail, analytical thinking
    

### 💻 **Tools and Technologies**

- **Forensic Tools** - _Tools and techniques for acquiring data from iOS devices._
    
- **iOS Devices** - _Understanding the architecture and forensically sound analysis of iOS devices._
