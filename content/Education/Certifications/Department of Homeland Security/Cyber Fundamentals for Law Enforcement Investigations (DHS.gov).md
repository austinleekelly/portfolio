## 📌 Summary

_The Cyber Fundamentals for Law Enforcement Investigations course offers foundational knowledge on digital evidence handling and cyber investigations, focusing on communication concepts, evidence sources, and investigative techniques used in law enforcement._

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** Cyber Fundamentals for Law Enforcement Investigations
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:** [Department of Homeland Security](https://www.dhs.gov/)
    
- **📆 Date Earned:** 12/29/2017
    
- **📜 Certification Level:** Intermediate
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **Computing Communication Concepts** – _Learn the basics of how devices communicate and transfer data._
    
- **Digital Evidence Handling** – _Identify digital evidence sources and best practices for handling it during investigations._
    
- **Cyber Investigation Techniques** – _Apply methods to examine applications for target information and support investigations._
    

## 🚀 Skills Acquired

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:** Digital forensics, cyber investigation techniques, evidence handling
    
- **Soft Skills:** Analytical thinking, attention to detail
    

### 💻 **Tools & Technologies**

- **Digital Evidence Tools** – _Techniques to identify, extract, and preserve digital evidence._
    
- **Cyber Investigation Tools** – _Tools used for analyzing applications and communication data._
