## 📌 Summary

_The Mobile and Device Security course introduces the basics of mobile device technologies, their security implications, and key considerations for securing and managing mobile devices. It covers threats, remediation methods, and forensic investigations related to mobile devices, as well as privacy concerns linked to social media._

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** Mobile and Device Security
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:**  [Department of Homeland Security](https://www.dhs.gov/)
    
- **📆 Date Earned:** 12/28/2017
    
- **📜 Certification Level:** Basic
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **Mobile Device Technology** – _Understand the components and architectures of mobile devices and how to secure them._
    
- **Threats & Remediation** – _Learn about historical and current mobile device threats and methods for remediation._
    
- **Forensic Investigations** – _Gain foundational knowledge for conducting mobile device forensic investigations._

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:** Mobile device security, forensic investigations, threat remediation
