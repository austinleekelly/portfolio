## 📌 Summary

_This course provides foundational knowledge in offensive and defensive network operations, focusing on how cyber operations are conducted. It covers U.S. government doctrine for network operations, network attack methodologies, and the tactics used to plan for, detect, and defend against network attacks._

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** Offensive and Defensive Network Operations
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:** [Department of Homeland Security](https://www.dhs.gov/)
    
- **📆 Date Earned:** 12/28/2017
    
- **📜 Certification Level:** Basic
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **U.S. Government Network Operations** – _Understand the background and doctrine for U.S. government network operations._
    
- **Offensive & Defensive Operations** – _Learn how offensive and defensive network operations are conducted._
    
- **Network Attack Detection & Defense** – _Identify offensive and defensive phases for planning, exploitation, and defending against network attacks._
    

## 🚀 Skills Acquired

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:** Network attack planning, detection techniques, defensive methodologies
    
- **Soft Skills:** Strategic thinking, problem-solving
    

### 💻 **Tools & Technologies**

- **Cyber Operations Tools** – _Tools and methodologies for offensive and defensive cyber operations._
    
- **Network Security Tools** – _Techniques for detecting and defending against network attacks._
