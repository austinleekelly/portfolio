### 📌 Summary
_This self-paced course explores the strengths, weaknesses, and security implications of cloud computing. It focuses on identifying and mitigating technical and operational risks while aligning with government standards for secure cloud adoption. The course concludes with a real-world case study of how the Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) uses cloud solutions securely._

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **Cloud Risk Awareness** - _Identify both advantages and vulnerabilities of cloud-based data storage and computing._
    
- **Security Strategy Implementation** - _Develop front-end risk mitigation strategies for operational and technical cloud environments._
    
- **Government Compliance** - _Understand regulatory and security requirements for cloud computing in federal contexts._
    
- **Case Study Application** - _Analyze how DISA leverages cloud services securely._
