## 📌 Summary

_The Critical Infrastructure Protection course provides essential knowledge on defending the United States' critical infrastructure against cybersecurity threats, covering key policies, risks, and roles in cybersecurity._

## 📂 Certification Overview

- **🏆 Certification:** 101 - Critical Infrastructure Protection
    
- **🎓 Issuing Organization:** [Department of Homeland Security](https://www.dhs.gov/)
    
- **📆 Date Earned:** 12/28/2017
    
- **📜 Certification Level:** Basic
    

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- **Critical Infrastructure Definition** - _Understand what constitutes critical infrastructure and its importance._
    
- **Cybersecurity Threats** - _Identify and analyze cybersecurity threats to critical infrastructure._
    
- **Cybersecurity Policies and Roles** - _Learn about U.S. cybersecurity policies and the roles of federal agencies like DHS._
    

## 📘 Curriculum Overview

Explores critical infrastructure, common cybersecurity threats, and ongoing protections in place. Highlights the role of DHS and other agencies in safeguarding vital systems.

### 🛠 **Core Skills**

- **Technical Skills:** Cybersecurity policy knowledge, threat identification, risk assessment
