### 📌 Summary

_This foundational course introduces the implementation and decision-making aspects of Continuous Monitoring as a Service (CMaaS) within DHS Component networks. It’s designed for federal employees involved in CDM initiatives to better understand how CMaaS supports security visibility and threat detection through centralized monitoring tools._

## 🎯 Key Learning Outcomes

- Understand the role of CMaaS in the Continuous Diagnostics and Mitigation (CDM) Program
    
- Identify CMaaS components and how they integrate into DHS network infrastructure
    
- Gain foundational knowledge of how CMaaS enhances real-time security monitoring
