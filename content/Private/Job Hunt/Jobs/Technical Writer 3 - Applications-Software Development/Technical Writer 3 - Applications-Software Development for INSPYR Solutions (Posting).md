**Job Title: Technical Writer 3 - Applications/Software Development  
Location: Remote  
Rate: $50/hour  
****Duration:** Ongoing Contract  
**Work Requirements:** US Citizen, Green Card Holder, or Authorized to Work in the US

We are seeking two experienced **Technical Writer 3** professionals to support high-level technical writing needs within IT development teams. The selected candidates will perform advanced writing work, coordinating the composition, organization, and editing of compiled technical information. This role operates under limited supervision with significant latitude for independent judgment and initiative.

Responsibilities:

- Develop and maintain user documentation, technical documentation, and project process documentation across various IT project teams.

- Compose, review, and edit technical documents, materials, and reports.

- Collaborate with subject matter experts to research topics and tailor content for specific audiences.

- Translate complex technical information into clear, user-friendly content suitable for both technical and non-technical audiences.

- Ensure all documentation adheres to internal style guides and industry standards.

- Regularly update existing documentation to reflect evolving processes and systems.

- Recommend revisions or changes in scope, format, or content after reviewing edited materials.

- Coordinate the integration of graphics and overall production of documentation deliverables.

- Write various types of technical content including manuals, reports, brochures, and knowledge base articles for diverse use cases.

- Understand and document user-facing procedures, ensuring a logical sequence and clear usability.

- Consult with internal teams to gather information and support documentation development.

- Research and develop best practices for presenting and organizing technical information.

- Provide expertise in translating application and user requirements into clear and concise documentation.

- Plan and manage documentation review cycles and serve as the primary point of contact during review processes.

- Maintain organized records and version control of documentation and revisions.

Qualifications:

- Strong ability to communicate complex information clearly and effectively.

- Experience working independently and managing multiple documentation projects simultaneously.

- Broad understanding of application development and technical systems.

- Demonstrated experience in a senior-level technical writing role, preferably supporting IT or software development projects.
