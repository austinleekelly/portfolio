Technical Writer Job Description

Accounting Seed is a rapidly growing SaaS-based accounting solution built on the Salesforce.com platform. Our software simplifies the accounting process for thousands of users in a diverse set of industries across the globe. We are an employee-first, fully remote employer looking to add to our entrepreneurial, passionate team.

**Who we are looking for:**

We are seeking a detail-oriented and tech-savvy Technical Writer with Salesforce experience to join our Product team. In this role, you will be responsible for creating and maintaining high-quality documentation and managing the Knowledge Base. You’ll collaborate closely with Development, QA, Product and Support teams to ensure documentation is accurate, updated and user-friendly.

The ideal candidate will have proven experience in a technical field, such as engineering, software, or accounting and a demonstrated capability of documenting business processes. The ability to evaluate and analyze complex information and a natural desire to help people understand that information is essential to this position.

**What You’ll Do:**

Develop, write, and maintain clear and concise end-user documentation that meets organizational standards which includes product features, FAQs, Troubleshooting guides and Release Notes.

Assist with maintaining Developer and API documentation.

Manage and maintain our Knowledge Base and manage content within Salesforce Knowledge.

Ensure articles are well-organized, searchable, and aligned with organizational standards.

Translate complex technical information into easy-to-understand content for both technical and non-technical audiences.

Work closely with subject matter experts (SMEs) to gather information, verify accuracy, and review documents.

Create visual aids such as diagrams, workflows, and screenshots to complement documentation.

Ensure consistency of style, tone, and formatting across all documentation.

Manage and organize documentation in centralized repositories (e.g., Salesforce Knowledge, Zendesk, Confluence, SharePoint).

Participate in product development meetings to stay informed on upcoming features and enhancements.

**Skills & Qualifications**

Required:

3+ years of experience as a technical writer, with at least 1 year of experience with Salesforce.

Previous experience writing documentation and procedural materials for multiple audiences.

Proven ability to quickly learn and understand complex topics.

Proficiency with Zendesk and Salesforce Knowledge for content creation and management.

Well-versed in authoring tools such as Confluence, MS Word or Google Docs.

Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with a keen eye for detail.

Strong organizational and project management abilities. Proven ability to handle multiple projects simultaneously, with an eye for prioritization.

Preferred:

Basic or working knowledge of Accounting.

Experience working in an Agile/Scrum environment.
