Join a mission-focused team driving clear, user-friendly documentation that directly impacts customer experience and marketing success. **About the Role**

We’re looking for a detail-driven **Technical Writer with Learning & Development (L&D) insight** to support cross-functional teams in delivering accurate, engaging documentation and training materials. You’ll work across Member, Provider, and Client portals—collaborating with teams to simplify complex information, develop user guides, and support portal training adoption efforts.

- Create and maintain user-facing documents: user guides, training decks, FAQs, and portal documentation- Format and structure documents for clarity; capture screenshots and design visual aids- Review and validate documentation for technical accuracy, consistency, and style- Update documentation based on new features or evolving business needs- Support L&D initiatives: help coordinate and deliver training resources- Organize content inputs from stakeholders to streamline documentation workflows- Collaborate with internal teams to train end users on new functionality- Independently research and resolve unclear technical topics- Thrive in a fast-paced, agile team with shifting priorities- Maintain a positive, solution-oriented approach when collaborating cross-functionally

  

**Success Metrics**

- Timely delivery of accurate, user-friendly documentation and training materials- High user satisfaction with portal documentation and training resources- Demonstrated adaptability to shifting requirements and feedback- Effective collaboration with CX, Marketing, and Product stakeholders

  

**Who You Are**

- **Experienced:** 3+ years in technical writing, documentation, or training roles- **Communicative:** Strong command of grammar, clarity, and tone- **Self-sufficient:** Comfortable working with limited input and managing ambiguity- **Tech-Savvy:** Proficient with Microsoft Word, Adobe, PDFs, and MS Office Suite- **Collaborative:** You’re comfortable liaising across multiple teams- **Bonus points** for L&D background or healthcare/dental industry experience- Writing samples strongly preferred
