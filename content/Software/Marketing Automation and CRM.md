## 📇 CRM and Lead Management

- **HubSpot** - Managed contact records, automated emails, and tracked leads for B2B marketing campaigns for [[Clients and Projects/index|multiple freelance clients]] and internally.  
- **Zoho CRM** - Used for small business pipeline management and automation rules tailored to [[Clients and Projects/index|multiple freelance client]] needs and internally.

---

## ✉️ Email Marketing and Automation

- **Klaviyo** - Designed ecommerce email flows like abandoned cart, post-purchase, and welcome series for a freelance ecommerce client. 
- **Mailchimp** - Managed newsletters and promotional campaigns for small businesses and nonprofits while [[Owner at Fancy Fox Creative|running my design studio]].  
- **ActiveCampaign** - Built behavior-based automations, conditional logic flows, and tagging systems for affiliate marketing clients.  
- **Omnisend** - Used for product campaigns and integrated SMS/email sequences for freelance ecommerce clients.  
- **Campaign Monitor** - Deployed newsletter and event-driven campaigns with advanced segmentation for affiliate marketing clients.  
- **GetResponse** - Built landing pages and lead magnet funnels with autoresponder sequences for affiliate marketing clients.

## 🧲 Lead Capture and Outreach

- **OptinMonster** - Created exit intent popups and embedded forms connected to email platforms for entreprenurial projects.  
- **Lemlist** - Used for personalized cold outreach with dynamic content, including video and images for obtaining freelance clients and for [[tags/Cold-Email|several freelance clients]].  
- **OutreachBin** - Managed multiple sender inboxes and email warm-up during outbound campaigns for [[tags/Cold-Email|several freelance clients]]..  
- **LeadFWD** - Used for personalized cold outreach with dynamic content for entrepreneurial ventures.

## 💬 Customer Messaging and Support

- **Intercom** - Used for live chat, onboarding flows, and proactive support messages in SaaS setups.  
- **Zendesk** - Managed support tickets and knowledge base articles for structured customer service.  
- **Freshdesk** - Deployed self-serve help centers and handled multi-channel ticket management.  
- **Tawk.to** - Embedded on client websites for 24/7 live chat and visitor engagement.
