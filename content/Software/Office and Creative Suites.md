## 🧾 Office and Productivity Suites

- **Microsoft Office / Office 365** - Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, OneNote.  Almost daily use for productivity in all full-time and [[Clients and Projects/index|freelance]] roles. Troubleshooting almost daily for [[Work Experience/index|full-time]] roles.
- **Google Workspace** - Docs, Sheets, Slides, Gmail, Drive - used for team collaboration and cloud access for several [[Clients and Projects/index|freelance]]  clients. Setup and managed for a few as well.  
- **LibreOffice** - Open-source alternative for word processing and spreadsheets. Used as daily driver for several years.
- **Zoho Office Suite** - Cloud-based document creation and management for SMB clients.

## 🎨 Creative and Design Tools

- **Adobe Creative Suite** - Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, Premiere Pro - used across marketing and client projects.  
- **Canva** - Fast mockups, social media content, and templates for lightweight design tasks for several freelance clients and entrepreneurial projects.
